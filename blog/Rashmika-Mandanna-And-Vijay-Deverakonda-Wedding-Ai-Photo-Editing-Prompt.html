Respectful Creation: Ethical Considerations

When creating AI images of real celebrities, especially in a personal context like a wedding, it's important to be respectful:

  • Admiration, Not Misrepresentation: Create images as fan art and expressions of admiration, not to spread rumors or claim they are real. Clearly label them as AI-generated.
  • Privacy Boundaries: Avoid creating overly intimate or inappropriate scenes. Keep it tasteful and celebratory.
  • Disclose AI Use: Always use hashtags like #AIFanArt or #AIGenerated when sharing online. This prevents misinformation and gives context.
  • Celebrate Positively: Focus on the joy and beauty of the imagined celebration, respecting both stars' real-life privacy and choices.
