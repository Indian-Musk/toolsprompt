Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a prompt?

Browse our marketplace, select a prompt you like, click "Buy" and complete the payment via Razorpay. Once purchased, you'll get immediate access to the full prompt text.

How do I become a seller?

Create an account, go to your dashboard, click "Sell Your Prompts", fill in your prompt details, set a price, and upload a demo image. Your prompt will be listed for buyers to purchase.

When do I get paid as a seller?

Payouts are processed monthly for amounts ₹500 or more. You can request payouts through your dashboard. Minimum payout amount is ₹100.

Can I get a refund?

Due to the digital nature of prompts, all sales are final. If you encounter technical issues, contact us within 7 days for assistance.

How do I report copyright infringement?

Send a DMCA notice to copyright@toolsprompt.com with details of the copyrighted work and the infringing content. We'll respond within 2-5 business days.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments via Razorpay which supports UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular wallets like Paytm, Google Pay, and PhonePe.
