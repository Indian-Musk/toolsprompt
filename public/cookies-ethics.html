Cookies Policy

This policy explains how ToolsPrompt uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our websites and how we use them.

What are cookies? Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • To enable essential website functionality
  • To analyze site usage and improve performance
  • To remember your preferences
  • To deliver personalized content
  • To support our marketing efforts

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions made by you.

Analytics Cookies

Allow us to count visits and traffic sources to measure and improve site performance.

Marketing Cookies

Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time. Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience.

AI Ethics Policy

At ToolsPrompt, we are committed to developing and deploying artificial intelligence responsibly. This policy outlines our ethical principles and practices.

Our Commitment: We believe AI should augment human capabilities, promote fairness, and be developed with consideration for its societal impact.

Core Ethical Principles

Human-Centered

AI systems should be designed to assist and augment human capabilities, not replace human judgment in critical areas.

Fairness

We strive to identify and mitigate biases in AI systems to ensure fair treatment across diverse user groups.

Transparency

We aim to make our AI systems' capabilities and limitations clear to users.

Privacy

We implement strong privacy protections and give users control over their data.

Safety

AI systems should be secure and reliable throughout their operational lifetime.

Accountability

We maintain human oversight of AI systems and establish clear accountability.

Our Implementation Practices

  • Bias Mitigation: Regular audits of our AI models to identify and address potential biases
  • Human Oversight: Maintaining human review for sensitive AI applications
  • Explainability: Developing interfaces that help users understand AI-generated content
  • Content Safeguards: Implementing filters to prevent harmful content generation
  • Continuous Monitoring: Tracking AI system performance and societal impact

Ethical AI Development Process

Our development lifecycle incorporates ethical considerations at every stage:

  1. Problem Definition: Assessing the ethical implications of new AI applications
  2. Data Collection: Ensuring diverse, representative data sources with proper consent
  3. Model Training: Implementing bias detection and mitigation techniques
  4. Testing: Rigorous evaluation for fairness, safety, and robustness
  5. Deployment: Clear documentation of system capabilities and limitations
  6. Monitoring: Ongoing assessment of real-world performance and impact

Reporting Concerns

We encourage users to report any concerns about potential ethical issues with our AI systems:

Email: ethics@toolsprompt.com

We investigate all reports promptly and take appropriate action to address any identified issues.
