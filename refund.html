Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

Quick Summary: Digital products (AI prompts) are non-refundable after download. For technical issues, we offer support within 7 days of purchase.

1. Policy Overview

At tools prompt, we strive to ensure a fair and transparent marketplace for both buyers and sellers. This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which refunds, cancellations, and chargebacks are handled.

By purchasing or selling AI prompts on our platform, you agree to be bound by this policy. We encourage you to read this policy carefully before making any purchase.

2. For Buyers - Purchasing AI Prompts

2.1 Digital Product Nature

All prompts sold on tools prompt are digital products. Due to the nature of digital goods, once a prompt has been downloaded, copied, or accessed, it cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense.

2.2 Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are considered only in the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: The prompt file is corrupt, incomplete, or cannot be accessed within 24 hours of purchase.
  • Misrepresentation: The prompt description significantly differs from what was delivered (e.g., advertised as a video prompt but delivered as an image prompt).
  • Duplicate Purchase: You accidentally purchased the same prompt twice within 24 hours.
  • Unauthorized Transaction: A purchase made without your consent (subject to verification).

2.3 Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted in the following cases:

  • Change of mind or buyer's remorse after viewing the prompt.
  • Failure to read the prompt description before purchase.
  • Results from the prompt did not meet personal expectations (AI results vary by platform and settings).
  • Purchase was made more than 7 days ago.
  • You have already copied or downloaded the prompt content.
  • You violated our Terms of Service.

2.4 Refund Request Process

1

Contact Support

Email us at support@toolsprompt.com with your order ID, prompt ID, and reason for refund.

2

Provide Details

Include screenshots or evidence of the issue (if applicable). This helps us process your request faster.

3

Review & Response

We will review your request within 2-3 business days and notify you of the decision.

4

Refund Processing

If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Refund Timeline: Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the purchase date. Requests after this period will not be considered.

3. For Sellers - Listing & Payouts

3.1 Listing Cancellation

As a seller, you may remove or cancel a prompt listing at any time from your dashboard. However:

  • If a prompt has already been purchased, the sale will be honored and you will receive the earnings.
  • You cannot remove a prompt that has active pending refund disputes.
  • We reserve the right to remove or suspend listings that violate our policies.

3.2 Disputed Sales

If a buyer initiates a refund request for your prompt:

  • We will notify you and review the dispute.
  • If the refund is approved, the sale amount will be reversed from your pending balance.
  • If the refund is denied, you will retain the earnings.
  • Repeated refund disputes may affect your seller status.

3.3 Seller Payment Reversals

In cases of fraudulent activity, policy violations, or chargebacks, we reserve the right to:

  • Hold or reverse pending seller payouts.
  • Suspend your seller account pending investigation.
  • Deduct the disputed amount from your available balance.
Important: Sellers are responsible for ensuring their prompts are accurately described. Misleading descriptions may result in refunds being issued against your sales.

4. Order Cancellation

4.1 Before Payment Completion

If you have not completed the payment through Razorpay:

  • Simply close the payment window - no order is created.
  • No charges will be applied to your account.

4.2 After Payment, Before Prompt Access

If you've paid but haven't yet viewed/copied the prompt:

  • Contact support within 2 hours of purchase.
  • We may cancel the order and issue a full refund.
  • Once the prompt page is viewed or the prompt is copied, refunds are no longer available.

4.3 Recurring Subscription Cancellations

If you have subscribed to any recurring service (not applicable for one-time prompt purchases):

  • Cancellations can be made from your dashboard settings.
  • No refunds for the current billing cycle - you will retain access until the end of the paid period.
  • Future cycles will not be charged after cancellation.

5. Chargeback Policy

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or payment provider:

  • Your tools prompt account will be temporarily suspended pending investigation.
  • You will be required to provide evidence supporting your claim.
  • If the chargeback is found to be invalid, your account will be permanently banned and legal action may be pursued.
  • We reserve the right to suspend accounts with excessive chargeback activity.
Important: Filing a fraudulent chargeback is a violation of our Terms of Service and may result in permanent account suspension and legal action.

6. Payment Gateway Issues

We use Razorpay as our payment processor. If you experience payment issues:

  • Payment Failed: If your payment fails but your account was charged, Razorpay will automatically reverse the transaction within 5-7 business days.
  • Double Charged: If you were charged twice for the same order, contact support with both transaction IDs for reversal.
  • Pending Transactions: Some banks may show pending transactions for up to 24 hours. Wait before requesting a refund.
Secure Payments: All payments are processed through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this Refund and Cancellation Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

In the event of material changes, we will notify users via email or through a notice on our website. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
